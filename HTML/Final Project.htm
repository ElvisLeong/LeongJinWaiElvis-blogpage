Final Project

For my final project, I've decided to tackle the oh so ever popular Useless machine.
Much like myself, when pushed or "activated", it will resist and turn itself off.

The most basic of "useless machines" consists of a switch to trigger it and a way to turn itself off, like a lever of some kind that can acuate the swith, all housed in a box of some kind.

The essential parts needed for such a project are:
Materials Quantity
Wood As much as needed to make a box lmao
Motors At least 2, one to move the lid and another to move the lever. Depending on whether there are gonna be any other additions, this number might change.
Arduino uno 1, to controll the whole circuit.
Toggle switch At least 1. Again, depending on whether there are gonna be more additions, more might be needed
Wires As many as is needed to connect everything.
